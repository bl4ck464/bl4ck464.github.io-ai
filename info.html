Foundational Definitions

The Biological Inspiration

Artificial systems model themselves after biological networks, optimizing numeric weights through layered nodes to map inputs directly to highly accurate predictive classifications.

The Mathematical Reality

Stripped of metaphor, it is pure multidimensional matrix multiplication driven by calculus. Backpropagation calculates loss gradients, moving functions toward global minima.
